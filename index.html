South County, Saint Louis, MO

I was born and raised in South Saint Louis County, MO. I graduated from Mehlville High School in 2009.

Columbia, MO

After high school, I attended the University of Missouri in Columbia, MO. I majored in business administration.

Starbucks

During my time at Mizzou, I worked at Starbucks.

Shrewsbury, MO

After I graduated from Mizzou, I moved back to the St. Louis area and settled into an apartment in Shrewbury, MO.

Marquette Group

My first business position out of college was as an Operations Associate at a marketing agency, Marquette Group. Here is where I first had the idea of a future career as an IT professional.

Soulard, Saint Louis, MO

My first move into St. Louis City was to the Soulard neighborhood. We lived in a quaint, historical apartment right in the middle of the main stretch of population, from 2016-2018.

Maritz

After a good stretch at Marquette Group, I got the opportunity to move into a new position at Maritz, a large company with a great reputation in the St. Louis area, as a business analyst in 2017. After about a year and a half, I got another opportunity to become a Product Owner in the IT/Engineering department on an Agile team. I am currently working in this position today.

Lindenwood Park, Saint Louis, MO

In 2018, it was time to plant roots in the City of St. Louis. We bought a house in Lindenwood Park, a quiet neighborhood in south Saint Louis city.

Launchcode and What's Next

In winter of 2019, I was accepted and began the Launchcode journey. As for future career goals and what's next after this course, I hope to continue to grow with my current career and use my skillset from LC101 to perhaps work closer with or as a developer or move into a TPM role. I am excited for the future!
